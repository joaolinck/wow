Warrior

Masters of melee combat, warriors excel in heavy armor, wielding massive weapons with precision. They endure fierce battles, relying on strength and endurance to lead their allies to victory.

Hunter

Hunters are expert marksmen and survivalists. Skilled with ranged weapons, they form bonds with animals and track their prey relentlessly. Masters of traps and camouflage, they strike from a distance with deadly precision.

Paladin

Paladins are holy knights, blending swordsmanship with divine magic. They serve as protectors, shielding their allies from harm while delivering righteous justice to their foes, guided by unwavering faith.

Priest

Priests are devoted healers and wielders of holy magic. They can mend wounds, shield allies, and vanquish the undead. Additionally, they delve into shadow magic, tapping into dark powers for damage and control, balancing the light and darkness within them.

Death Knight

Death Knights are fearsome warriors raised from the dead by the Lich King, wielding necromantic powers and unholy strength. Bound to frost, blood, and unholy magic, they are resilient melee fighters, commanding undead minions and diseases to devastate their enemies.

Mage

Mages are powerful spellcasters, harnessing arcane magic to cast devastating spells. They manipulate frost, fire, and the arcane to decimate foes or protect their allies. Highly intelligent and skilled, mages can also alter time, teleport, and create powerful magical barriers.
